Hello again friends

Hola amigos, I'm back with another whirl of whimsy. I slacked, but we're simply so back. My brother and my bestest friend both left Calgary for new adventures recently, so I've been a bit sad. The good kind of sad though. "What is grief, if not evidence of love?" and whatnot. Needless to say, I've been especially somber as of late. Let's explore my psyche together, shall we?

I'm kinda bad at wanting things

I'm in a weird situationship with my desire. On one hand I ignore its asks and resist sacrificing for it. On the other, it refuses to articulate itself and contradictions stack up quickly when it does. I don't really know why I want so many contradictory things.

I've recently been thinking of "wanting" as a skill to be practiced. And I worry that I've let that muscle atrophy a little. Wanting things, properly, is difficult & vulnerable for me. It exposes who I am and I struggle with some resistance to that. I'm not entirely sure how to train this muscle, but I think it's a worthwhile pursuit.

Constantly watched, easily embarrassed

Do we live in a digital surveillance state? Between the permanence of recorded media, the rise of cringe culture, & performative everything, I feel as though I'm being watched even when I'm not. I mentioned this idea to my friend D & she noted that people seem much more easily embarrassed than they used to be. Candidly, this feels true for me too.

I don't even have much more to comment. I perceive a strangely persistent observation of my actions, and anything remotely interesting about my life requires overcoming it. I suppose this is me announcing a new side quest for myself.

Hey these were interesting

📚 Surrounded by idiots is a book I read recently. It's an interesting framework for personality, and I feel as though I learned to have more grace after reading it.

🎵 Look too easy - Graham is a great song. I have had it on repeat a probably too much recently.

🎬 The Hidden Structure in Magic That Created the Archetypes is a video about Magic: The Gathering that is secretly just game design theory. In general this podcast analyzes Magic through the lens of broader game design and if that's your kind of nerd then I would highly recommend.

📎 The Missing Missing Reasons is an essay about estrangement and the differing perspectives of estranged parents and estranged children. It's a little hopeless in its tone, so I struggle to fully resonate with it, but I think it's an interesting topic explored well here.
